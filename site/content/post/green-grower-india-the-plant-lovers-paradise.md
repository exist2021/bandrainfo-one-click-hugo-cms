<p>A plant lover&#8217;s paradise! Established in Nov of 98, we have since been inspiring the people of Mumbai into embracing the wonders of nature into their lives by catering to all their &#8220;green&#8221; needs.</p>
<p>We boast of two beautiful retail Nurseries in the prominent suburbs of Bandra and a 25-Acre production farm at Karjat. What sets us apart from other Nurseries is our exceptional range of quality indoor and outdoor plants and our specialized garden services provided to you by our well trained &amp; knowledgeable staff of 80 strong.</p>
<p>We also provide garden tools and accessories like plastic and ceramic pots, rocks and pebbles, soil, organic fertilizers, organic pesticides, flower and vegetable seeds.</p>
<p><b>Products &amp; Services</b></p>
<p><b>Retail and Wholesale of Plants</b></p>
<p>We host an exceptional range of quality indoor and outdoor plants, along with a wide selection of garden accessories including tools, pots, rocks and pebbles, soil, manures and other organic fertilizers, pesticides, flower and vegetable seeds.</p>
<p><b>Landscaping, Design and Consultancy</b></p>
<p>Green Grower also specializes in landscaping- right from the conceptualization stage to the execution of the project. We undertake projects that involve beautifying a given area to its maximum potential keeping in mind the diverse requirements of customers.</p>
<p><b>Gift-a-Plant</b></p>
<p>Gift-A-Plant &#8211; An ideal gift for birthdays, anniversaries or even corporate gifting purposes. You can choose from a range of gifts, for example, our excellent Bonsai collection of more than a 100 plants, miniature landscapes, terrariums (bottle gardens), plant baskets, etc. We deliver anywhere within Mumbai with adequate notice.</p>
<p><b>Rent-a-Plant</b></p>
<p>Rent-A-Plant offers customers the opportunity to rent plants on a daily, monthly or yearly contract. This scheme is inclusive of the services of our gardeners who visit customer premises three times a week for regular watering, fertilizing, pruning and general maintenance of the plants hired on a yearly contract.</p>
<p><b>Tree Transplanting</b></p>
<p>An environmentally friendly way to deal with problem trees, this is a specialized service and we can proudly say that we have successfully undertaken many a transplanting jobs with very encouraging results.</p>
<p><b>Mali Service</b></p>
<p>Mali Service is a newly introduced service that will benefit patrons who are unable to carry out laborious maintenance work such as potting, fertilizer application, spraying etc.</p>
<p><b>Green Wall / Vertical Garden</b></p>
<p>A new service introduced by Green Grower, where we assist customers in setting up Vertical Gardens/ Green Walls to enhance their inner and outer living spaces. The main aim of setting up green walls is to blend nature with the existing ambience.</p>
