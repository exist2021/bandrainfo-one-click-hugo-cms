<p>Cottonworld is India’s first store dedicated to natural clothing. The first store was set up in 1987 in a leafy lane in Colaba, Mumbai, and to this day, has loyalists who have been frequenting the store since inception.</p>
<p>Cottonworld has since grown to over 20 stores in Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Chennai, Goa, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune and Gurgaon.</p>
<p>With its clean lines, soothing colours and natural fabrics, Cottonworld has an appeal that transcends generations.</p>
<p>Cotton world is a world of natural fabrics &#8211; Comfort clothing that put a piece of this planet on us &#8211; With love.</p>
<p>Cottonworld forged new cuts and colour all this while to enliven our statement of style.</p>
<p>It stays the brand of choice to those who love this World we are in, fashion and life.</p>
<p>Cottonworld is the canvas of creativity to us.</p>
<p>Cottonworld – Get dressed to Chill. Play your style, Naturally.</p>
