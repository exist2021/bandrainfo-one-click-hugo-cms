<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><img loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-10511 alignleft" src="https://i1.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/23f43f33-1d7e-47a5-8913-13dc82bfc8a1.jpg?resize=300%2C420&#038;ssl=1" alt="" width="300" height="420" data-recalc-dims="1" />Delving into the enchanting world of Tarot Cards, Shambhavi Chhugani has been an accomplished Tarot Reader for over 17, plus years and is also an expert in Colours Psychology, Vaastu, Space Cleansing, Switchwords, Meditation, Doodle Analysis, Healing through art and Dreams Interpretation. Apart from the ones listed her other area of expertise are as follows, for which she has also been awarded a certificate of recognizitions from various mentors and institutes they are namely, Dreams Awareness, Switchography, Runes Guidance &amp; Healing, First &amp; Second Degree Reiki, Basic Nyaas Yoga to name a few.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Shambhavi’s passion and dedication for Tarot cards was always limitless and the science associated with it has never ceased to enchant her. Learning more about Tarot cards and eventually growing in the process, she turned to be a perfect professional in the field serving </span><span class="s2">clientele including the who’s who of business and media fraternity, corporate houses to housewives, students and anyone who seeks her guidance</span><span class="s1">.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Climbing up the ladder of success Shambhavi made partners with big brands like JW Marriott, Renaissance, Oakwoad and Out of the Blue- Mumbai, where she facilitated in-house Tarot reading. Apart from these hotel chains, Shambhavi is also associated with corporates like Future group (Pantaloons), DLF, JLT , ORRA and Lavasa to name a few.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Shambhavi has also been a part of the prestigious ELLE Carnival, held in Mumbai to raise funds for breast cancer patients and has been written about in their magazine.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">The Year 2016, was very rewarding for Shambhavi, where she saw her work being acknowledged and she being Awarded as Grand Master in Tarot Prediction by Inspire.She was facilitated by actress Divya Dutta in NSCI Club Worli in November. A fortnight later she was again accredited with a WOW Personality Award which is Winners of Worthiness, in Excellence in Tarot. Its been a very rewarding experience for her, and her journey continues..</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">Along with doing Tarot reading sessions, she regularly hosts educative workshops on topics ranging from colour therapy to Doodle to Vaastu and more imparting valuable information and knowledge to all in attendance at schools, corporate hubs, nature camps, social meets and other venues.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1"><img loading="lazy" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-10517" src="https://i0.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG-20170109-WA0009.jpg?resize=500%2C250&#038;ssl=1" alt="" width="500" height="250" srcset="https://i0.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG-20170109-WA0009.jpg?resize=500%2C250&amp;ssl=1 500w, https://i0.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG-20170109-WA0009.jpg?resize=768%2C384&amp;ssl=1 768w, https://i0.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG-20170109-WA0009.jpg?resize=1024%2C512&amp;ssl=1 1024w, https://i0.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/IMG-20170109-WA0009.jpg?w=1080&amp;ssl=1 1080w" sizes="(max-width: 500px) 100vw, 500px" data-recalc-dims="1" />Adding more feathers to her cap, Shambhavi was recently invited to attend the Shop for a cause – Council For Cancer Care event, Hathautee Mumbai Book fair exhibition and The Great Indian Wedding in Melhua, Mumbai. She was also one of the speakers at ‘One with Nature- beyond academics in January 2014 on “Colours Psychology and Vasstu with Children”.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s2">Her work has been commended and covered by, both, national and local media and she has even contributed article to Bombay Times on “The Mysticism of Tarot” which garnered huge viewership.<span class="Apple-converted-space">  </span>She has also written articles on Doodles – Can we Interpret Them, How would Aura and Space Cleansing help you?, Vastu – Key to Happiness, Promenade – Lifestyle exhibition and Colour Therapy for various publications.</span></p>
<p class="p1"><span class="s1">She also conducts Tarot learning classes for all those who are interested in pursuing the science of prediction along with developing intuitive skills and interpreting cards.<span class="Apple-converted-space">   </span></span></p>
