<p>White Peacock Edge, a unique fashion house, is an idea born out of the need for well designed contemporary clothing for the cosmopolitan Indian woman.</p>
<p>Started by Ekta Mohanani, White Peacock Edge believes that every woman is beautiful and has an inherent style quotient, sometimes caught in the blizzard of everyday life, one tends to forget about taking care of themselves, it’s here that aspires to bridge the gap between passivity and exuberance.</p>
<p>Ekta is not formally trained in design, she is truly global in approach. She has an extensive knowledge of trends and an elegant taste in selection of exclusive stuff that make her store unique.</p>
<p><a href="https://i1.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/white-peacock2.jpg?ssl=1"><img loading="lazy" class="size-full wp-image-1500 aligncenter" alt="white peacock2" src="https://i1.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/white-peacock2.jpg?resize=598%2C399&#038;ssl=1" width="598" height="399" srcset="https://i1.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/white-peacock2.jpg?w=598&amp;ssl=1 598w, https://i1.wp.com/bandra.info/wp-content/uploads/2013/05/white-peacock2.jpg?resize=300%2C200&amp;ssl=1 300w" sizes="(max-width: 598px) 100vw, 598px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a></p>
<p>White Peacock Edge is a one stop shop for all your shopping needs. The White Peacock &amp; Edge collection are based on international fashion forecasts, keeping your wardrobe updated with the latest trends, high end customized to suit your body.</p>
<p>They have an in-house stylist who brings to you stylish, quality and affordable fashions. Their specialized and popular High End Collection under the Edge label ensures that clients are always immaculately dressed.</p>

<p>At White Peacock Edge it’s not about just buying a new outfit. It&#8217;s an experience that will change how you perceive shopping by making it personalized and special. It is for the woman you are &amp; the woman you want to be. This style potion is a tribute to the woman &amp; it will make heads turns, wherever you go.</p>
